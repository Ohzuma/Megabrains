Graphic Design

GRAPHIC DESIGN: Also known as communication design, is the art and practice of planning and pro- jecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. Designers arrange type, form and image on posters, advertisements, packages and other print- ed matter, as well as information visualizations and graphics for newspapers, magazines and websites.

OPPORTUNITIES:

Probably the most important benefit of being a graphic artist is the ability to ex- press your creativity, while still earning a great living. Every business needs a graphic artist. If a compa- ny or an organization is going to do marketing, then someone is going to design the marketing materials. As long as businesses keep growing and new ones keep appearing, then graphic designers or artists will always have plenty of work.
